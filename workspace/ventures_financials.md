## Key Financial Insights

1. **Hardware cost: $0.** Everything runs on John's existing Mac Studio (MLX-LM) + cheap cloud hosting ($5-15/month per venture).

2. **Fastest payback: Lead Gen (Venture 2).** 7-30 days to first dollar, 1-3 months payback. This is the cashflow engine that funds the others.

3. **Highest lifetime value: Micro-SaaS (Venture 3).** 70-90% gross margins after Year 1. Scales without linear effort.

4. **Most passive: SEO Sites (Venture 1).** Once ranking (Month 3-6), ~$30-300/site/month with ~0 ongoing work (quarterly refreshes only).

5. **Year 1 projection (Realistic, hybrid approach):** ~$1,000 total investment → ~$15,000-30,000 revenue → ~$14,000-29,000 net profit (Year 1).

6. **Year 2 projection (Realistic):** ~$30,000-60,000 net (no incremental investment needed — just domain renewals).

7. **Year 3 projection (Realistic):** ~$50,000-100,000+ net (portfolio compounds, SaaS grows).
