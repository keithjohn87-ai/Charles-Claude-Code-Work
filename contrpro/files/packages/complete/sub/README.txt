ContrPro Universal Sub Suite — Complete Tier
==============================================

Eight documents that protect a subcontractor across the full project
lifecycle: bid response, contract review, monthly billing, time-and-material
work, backcharge defense, certified payroll, joint-check arrangements, and
daily field reporting.

Sub-perspective companion to the GC suite. The GC suite covers what a GC
sends out; this suite covers what a sub responds with, signs, and defends.

==============================================
What you get
==============================================

1. Bid Package & Prequalification
   (bid-package-and-prequalification.html / .docx)
   15-section master template for responding to an ITB and submitting
   prequalification. Mirrors the framework of AIA A305.

2. Subcontract Review Checklist
   (subcontract-review-checklist.html / .docx)
   Clause-by-clause redline guide. Walks every Subcontract article with
   Accept / Redline / Walk-Away framing.

3. Sub Schedule of Values (G-703 style)
   (Sub_Schedule_of_Values.xlsx / .csv)
   7-tab workbook: Instructions, Project Info, SOV Setup, Pay App
   (current period), Pay App History (12 months), Stored Materials Log,
   CSI Reference.

4. T&M Tracker
   (TM_Tracker.xlsx / .csv)
   9-tab workbook for time-and-material billing: Labor Tickets, Equipment
   Tickets, Material Tickets, Subcontract Tickets, Invoice Roll-Up, Labor
   Rate Schedule with prevailing-wage support.

5. Backcharge Dispute Pack
   (backcharge-dispute-pack.html / .docx)
   Five letter templates (Notice of Objection, Demand for Itemization,
   Demand for Substantiation, Formal Protest, Lien & Bond Reservation)
   plus evidence preservation checklist and disposition tracker.

6. Certified Payroll & Prevailing Wage Guide
   (certified-payroll-and-prevailing-wage-guide.html / .docx)
   12-section operational guide for Davis-Bacon and state prevailing wage
   compliance.

   Companion: Certified Payroll Tracker
   (Certified_Payroll_Tracker.xlsx / .csv)
   WH-347-mirror workbook with Wage Determination, Workers Master,
   Weekly Payroll, and Statement of Compliance tabs.

7. Joint Check Agreement & Guide
   (joint-check-agreement-and-guide.html / .docx)
   3-way agreement template (GC + Sub + Supplier) plus three cover letters
   (request, notice, transmittal) and state-variation notes.

8. Daily Field Report Template & Guide
   (daily-field-report-template-and-guide.html / .docx)
   Printable DFR form, weekly roll-up form, foreman's pre-sign checklist.

   Companion: Daily Field Report Workbook
   (Daily_Field_Report.xlsx / .csv)
   33-tab workbook: Instructions, Project Info, Monthly Roll-Up, plus
   Day 01 through Day 31 (one tab per day for a month).

==============================================
File formats — your tools, our forms
==============================================

Every document ships in multiple formats:
  - .html — source of truth, open in any browser, print to PDF
  - .docx — Word; opens in Microsoft Word, Apple Pages, LibreOffice,
            and uploads as-is to Google Docs (File → Open → upload)
  - .xlsx — Excel workbooks; open in Excel, Numbers, Google Sheets,
            LibreOffice Calc
  - .csv  — flat data for QuickBooks, Sage, FreshBooks import

==============================================
Important reminders
==============================================

These templates are tailored to common commercial-construction practice
and parallel AIA / ConsensusDocs form frameworks without reproducing
copyrighted forms. They are NOT legal advice.

Before first use on any new project type, new GC relationship, or any
prevailing-wage project, have construction counsel licensed in the state
of the Project review the template and the underlying facts.
