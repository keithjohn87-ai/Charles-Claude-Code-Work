ContrPro — SBA Bonus
====================================================

The SBA bonus folder shipped with every Complete-tier bundle. Four documents
covering everything a contractor needs to actually pursue + close an SBA loan.

WHAT'S IN HERE
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. SBA_Funding_Mastery_Guide
   Formats: .md (source) + .docx (Word)
   The end-to-end playbook. ~40 KB, 13 sections. Covers every SBA program
   (7(a) / 504 / Express / Microloan / CAPLines / Working Capital Pilot),
   construction-specific eligibility, what your application packet needs to
   contain, lender selection strategy, common rejection reasons, and the
   negotiation moves once you have an offer in hand.

2. Business_Plan_Template
   Formats: .md (source) + .docx (Word — fillable form)
   SBA-ready business plan template structured to what lenders look for first:
   executive summary, market, ops, financials, capital ask. Construction-
   tailored (not the generic SCORE outline). Fill in the .docx in Word, save
   as PDF for submission.

3. Financial_Projection_Templates
   Formats: .md (source) + .docx (narrative) + .xlsx (WORKING WORKBOOK)
   The .xlsx is the load-bearing format — an 8-tab working financial-projection
   workbook. Construction-tuned with work-in-progress + retainage receivables
   + progress-billing cadence baked into the cashflow model. Fill the ONE
   Assumptions tab and the entire 3-year P&L + cashflow + balance sheet +
   ratios + SBA sources-and-uses + lender summary auto-populate.

4. Lender_Comparison_Matrix
   Formats: .md (source) + .docx (printable) + .xlsx (working comparison)
   The .xlsx has 5 tabs: top SBA 7(a) lenders ranked by recent fiscal-year
   volume with construction-friendliness scoring, a 1-5 scoring worksheet for
   your shortlist, a call-notes log to compare offers, and source links for
   verifying current rankings on sba.gov.

HOW TO USE THESE TOGETHER
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Read SBA_Funding_Mastery_Guide.docx first — it tells you which SBA
     program to apply for + which lenders make sense for construction.
  2. Open Lender_Comparison_Matrix.xlsx + pick 5 lenders to call from the
     'Selection Worksheet' tab.
  3. Open Financial_Projection_Templates.xlsx + fill in the Assumptions tab.
     The Lender Summary tab becomes the 1-page roll-up you send first to each
     lender.
  4. Fill in Business_Plan_Template.docx with the narrative + numbers from
     the projection.
  5. Submit your packet (business plan + projections + Lender Summary) to the
     top 3 lenders on your shortlist.

IMPORTANT REMINDERS
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

These are templates + references. Not legal/financial advice. ContrPro is not
an SBA-approved lender, broker, or financial advisor. Verify current SBA
program rules + lender approval volumes on sba.gov before applying. Have your
CPA review the financial projections before submitting.
