ContrPro General Contractor Suite — Complete Tier
====================================================

Eight contract + execution templates and three working workbooks that cover
the GC's job from bid through closeout.

====================================================
What you get — 8 docs + 3 workbooks
====================================================

LEGAL & EXECUTION TEMPLATES (HTML + DOCX, AIA-depth)
  - Master Subcontract Agreement (GC ↔ Sub)
  - GMP Agreement (Guaranteed Maximum Price)
  - Cost-Plus Agreement + Delivery Method Guide
  - Application for Payment (G-702/G-703 procedural reference)
  - Change Order Request & Authorization
  - RFI / Submittal / Transmittal Templates
  - Schedule Baseline & Lookahead Guide
  - Closeout / Substantial Completion / Punch List

WORKING WORKBOOKS (XLSX)
  - GC_Bid_Estimator.xlsx
      Prime-contract bid roll-up. 8 tabs: Trade Bids by CSI division,
      Self-Perform, General Conditions (Div 01), Allowances, Bid Summary
      with OH+P+Bond+Tax to final bid number.

  - GC_Application_for_Payment.xlsx
      Monthly billing to the Owner. 8 tabs: Project Info, SOV Setup,
      G-703 Pay App (Continuation Sheet), G-702 Summary (Application &
      Certificate), 12-month Pay App History, Stored Materials Log.

  - GC_Project_Operations.xlsx
      CONSOLIDATED workbook for execution-phase tracking. 9 tabs with a
      shared Project Info header driving every other tab:
        * RFI Log
        * Submittal Log
        * Schedule / 3-Week Lookahead
        * Daily Field Report Log (row-per-day)
        * Punch List
        * Meeting Minutes
      Enter project name / owner / GC ONCE — every tab pulls from there.

====================================================
The 3-workbook split — WHY this and not 8 workbooks
====================================================

Workbooks are split by PHASE + USER, not by document type:

  - Bid Estimator = bid phase, used by Estimator
  - Application for Payment = monthly billing phase, used by Accounting/PM
  - Project Operations = execution phase, used by PM (RFI/Submittal/Schedule/
    DFR/Punch List/Meeting Minutes all share one Project Info header)

This is deliberately NOT 40 different workbooks where you fill in project
name 40 times. The Operations workbook is the consolidation — same user,
same workflow, shared header.

File formats — your tools, our forms
====================================================

  - .html — source of truth, open in any browser, print to PDF

            uploads as-is to Google Docs
  - .xlsx — Excel; opens in Excel, Numbers, Google Sheets, LibreOffice

====================================================
Important reminders
====================================================

These templates parallel common commercial-construction practice and AIA
form frameworks without reproducing copyrighted material. They are NOT
legal advice. Before first use on any new project type or new owner/
architect relationship, have construction counsel review the tailored
documents. Bid estimating defaults are industry-average; calibrate against
your shop history before relying on them.
