ContrPro Plumbing Trade Pack — Complete Tier
====================================================

Trade-specific delta on top of the Universal Sub Suite for commercial
plumbing contractors. Full division scope (water distribution, sanitary,
storm, gas, fixtures, water heaters) — field installation only, no
fabrication-shop pre-assembly.

USE THIS WITH THE UNIVERSAL SUB SUITE.
This pack does NOT replicate the universal forms (Bid Package, Subcontract
Review, Sub SOV, T&M Tracker, Backcharge Dispute, Certified Payroll, Joint
Check, Daily Field Report). Those live in ../sub/ and apply to every trade
identically. This pack adds the trade-specific tools a plumbing contractor
needs ON TOP of the universal forms.

====================================================
What you get — 6 deliverables, 10 files
====================================================

1. Plumbing Bid Estimator
   (Plumbing_Bid_Estimator.xlsx)
   10-tab fixture-and-LF-based bid estimator. CSI Division 22 primary with
   PHCC/MCAA-style labor units. Tabs: Instructions, Bid Summary, Project Info,
   Wage Rates, Material Takeoff (75 line items pre-loaded), Labor Estimate
   (45 activity rows), Equipment, Subcontract, Premiums & Conditions, CSI
   Reference. Bid Summary rolls up by CSI division.

2. Site-Specific Plumbing Plan
   (site-specific-plumbing-plan.html / .docx)
   16-section project-specific compliance roadmap covering AHJ coordination,
   inspection sequence, cross-connection control, hydrostatic + pressure
   testing, disinfection, confined-space entry, trenching/excavation,
   hot work, hazmat (asbestos/lead/mold), multi-trade coordination,
   ICRA/occupied-building considerations, emergency response, stop-work
   triggers, signatures.

3. Pre-Construction Coordination Checklist
   (pre-construction-coordination-checklist.html / .docx)
   14-section pre-mobilization meeting agenda + minutes. Attendance roster,
   contract review, permit + AHJ confirmation, drawing + spec review,
   submittal schedule, long-lead tracking, site logistics, multi-trade
   coordination, inspection sequence, safety program, insurance/bonds/
   licenses, manpower forecast + first-day plan, action items, signatures.

4. Cross-Connection Control & Backflow Guide + Test Log
   (cross-connection-control-and-backflow-guide.html / .docx +
    Backflow_Test_Log.xlsx)
   10-section compliance guide + 6-tab test-log workbook.
     HTML: hazard mechanics, classification (high/low), device types
     (RPZ/DCV/PVB/SVB/AVB/AG), selection matrix by application,
     installation requirements, initial field test procedures
     (ASSE 1013/1015), annual testing + reporting, repair criteria,
     documentation, common errors.
     XLSX: Device Inventory, Initial Test Log, Annual Test Log, Repair Log,
     Reporting Summary (auto next-test-due calc).

5. Pipe Installation & Test Guide + Pressure Test Log
   (pipe-installation-and-test-guide.html / .docx +
    Pressure_Test_Log.xlsx)
   10-section field reference + 7-tab test-log workbook.
     HTML: material selection by system, joining methods (solder/press/
     threaded/solvent/crimp/welded/brazed/no-hub/fusion), hanger spacing
     (IPC 308 / UPC 314), pipe expansion, insulation, sleeves + firestop,
     pressure testing (IPC 312), gas testing (IFGC 406), disinfection
     (AWWA C651), common errors.
     XLSX: Test Schedule, Hydrostatic Test Log, DWV Test Log, Gas Pressure
     Test, Failed-Test Investigation, Reporting Summary.

6. Closeout & Inspection Compliance Guide + Punch List Log
   (closeout-and-inspection-compliance-guide.html / .docx +
    Punch_List_and_AsBuilt_Log.xlsx)
   10-section closeout-process reference + 6-tab punch/as-built workbook.
     HTML: 3-phase closeout timeline, final-inspection checklist,
     punch list management, substantial vs final completion, O&M manual
     contents, as-built drawings, warranty period, required submissions,
     lien releases + retention, common pitfalls.
     XLSX: Punch List (80-row running log with status validation),
     Closed Punch Archive, As-Built Documentation Checklist (standard
     sheets pre-populated), O&M Manual Index (12-section required-section
     index), Final Submissions Tracker.

====================================================
CSI MasterFormat Integration
====================================================

Every back-office tool in this pack is CSI-coded. The Bid Estimator uses
CSI Division 22 (Plumbing) as primary, with cross-references to:
  - 21 13 00  Fire-Suppression Sprinklers (typically separate trade)
  - 22 05 00  Common Work Results for Plumbing
  - 22 05 19  Meters and Gauges
  - 22 05 23  General-Duty Valves
  - 22 05 29  Hangers and Supports
  - 22 07 00  Plumbing Insulation
  - 22 08 00  Commissioning of Plumbing
  - 22 11 00  Facility Water Distribution
  - 22 13 00  Facility Sanitary Sewerage
  - 22 14 00  Facility Storm Drainage
  - 22 31-35  Domestic Water Softeners / Filters / Heaters
  - 22 40-47  Plumbing Fixtures + Emergency + Drinking Fountains
  - 22 60-67  Lab + Healthcare Gas / Vacuum Systems
  - 23 21 00  Hydronic Piping (cross-ref to mechanical)
  - 31 23 00  Excavation + Backfill (cross-ref to site)
  - 33 10 00  Water Utilities (cross-ref to site)
  - 33 30 00  Sanitary Sewerage Utilities (cross-ref to site)

The Bid Summary tab rolls up totals by CSI division so the bid integrates
cleanly into the GC's job-cost accounting AND into your monthly billing
via the Universal Sub Suite's Sub_Schedule_of_Values.xlsx.

====================================================
Field-only scope
====================================================

This trade pack is calibrated for field installation only. Fabrication-shop
pre-assembly (modular piping racks, pump skids built off-site, prefab
multi-fixture wall units) is out of scope and would require a separate
shop-prefab estimator with different rate structures, productivity
assumptions, and quality systems.

Full scope WITHIN field installation includes: domestic water distribution
(supply through fixtures), sanitary DWV (collection through building drain),
storm drainage (collection through building drain), natural gas piping
(meter through appliance shutoff), commercial plumbing fixtures (set + trim
+ supply + waste connections), commercial water heaters (set + connect),
backflow prevention (install + initial test arrangement). Laboratory and
healthcare gas systems are included where applicable; the
medical-gas-specific tools (NFPA 99 brazing under nitrogen purge, ASSE 6010
brazer requirements) are referenced but the SSPP can be tailored if your
project covers them.

====================================================
Important reminders
====================================================

These templates are tailored to common commercial-construction practice
and reference IPC 2024 / UPC 2024 / IFGC 2024 / NFPA 54 / NFPA 99 / AWWA
C651 / ASSE + CSA Backflow Standards / OSHA 29 CFR 1926 and 1910 without
reproducing copyrighted material. They are NOT legal advice and they are
NOT a substitute for the Master Plumber of Record's professional judgment,
the AHJ plumbing inspector's project-specific direction, the engineer of
record's structural / hazard review, or a state-licensed cross-connection
control specialist's certification.

Before first use on any new project, have construction counsel and your
master plumber review the tailored documents. Federal + national-model code
references are current as of January 2026 — verify against the latest
AHJ-adopted edition and any local amendments.
